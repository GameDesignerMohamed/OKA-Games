# DUEL PULSE

**Concept:** A 1v1 skill-duel arena where the session itself is a complete, shareable social moment — the direct inversion of Rec Room's freeform social sandbox with no owned loop.
**Player fantasy:** You are a clean, decisive duelist. Land 3 hits before they land 3 on you. Every second of every session is skill.

## Core Loop
Strafe to control angle, aim at the AI, fire pulse shots, dodge return fire. 3 hits to kill — sessions complete in under 90 seconds. Every duel is fully describable in one sentence: "Won 3-1, last dodge was clean."

## Key Mechanics
- **WASD strafe** — circle-strafing is the primary skill expression
- **Mouse aim + fire** — projectile with travel time, rewards lead-shooting
- **Shift dodge dash** (1.5s cooldown) — the core skill ceiling mechanic; a timed dodge is the defining high-skill moment
- **3-hit-kill** — short TTK keeps sessions completable and the score always memorable
- **BGM pressure escalation** — audio raises tension as HP drops, diegetic skill signal

## What's Built
The prototype proves the loop: spawn, strafe, shoot, dodge, KO in under 90 seconds. Full juice stack — bloom, screen shake, particle KO explosion, hit emissive flash, procedural Web Audio BGM that escalates with HP pressure. AI circle-strafes and fires on a randomized interval. The dash mechanic already creates a genuine "earned moment" — a near-miss dodge reads as a skill expression the player will want to describe.
